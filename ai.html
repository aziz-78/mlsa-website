Summary

A workshop covering the basic elements of AI and how these elements are used in more complex things The workshop was designed to provide insight into the different areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for the students of Computer Science. The focus was on the most up to date approaches and results in the field of artificial intelligence and introductory familiarity with the basic terminology and concepts of AI
