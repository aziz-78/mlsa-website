Digital Design Roadshow

  • Type: Design
  • Partner: Telenor
  • Date: 7-15 August, 2020

Summary

Digital Design Roadshow was a collaboration between Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors - Islamabad chapter and Telenor Pakistan. The main focus was to bring UI/UX development accessible to everyone. At Digital Design Roadshow, attendees learned from experienced speakers that are working with leading brands and companies, like Electronic Arts, Telenor Pakistan, KeepTruckin, IBM and more. This event had a total of 7 workshops covering topics like resolving UI challenges, modern design tools and prototyping.
